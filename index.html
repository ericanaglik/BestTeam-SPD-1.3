Who It's For

All You Need Is An Apple Watch And We Do The Rest

Download the app and grant ReccoMinder permissions to all data in HealthKit and notification access. From there, ReccoMinder will start giving you the reminders and reccomendations that you need.


How Does It Work?

Your Heart Rate Says A Lot About You

We all know that your heart rate can provide insights into your biological wellbeing. At ReccoMinder we take these insights and compare them to your Apple Watch HealthKit data to give our best reccommendations for things you need in the form of a timely notification.

Water Intake

ReccoMinder sends you daily notifications on the best time to drink water based upon your heart rate and how long it's been since your last intake. You can also set a daily water intake goal that ReccoMinder can help you achieve.

Exercise Time

It's hard to find the perfect time to exercise outdoors when so many factors come into play. ReccoMinder simplifies this decision for you by taking into account weather conditions, the UV index, and your heart rate to notify you when it's the right time.
